ElderAssist Brand Kit · Direction B (Bond)
==========================================

CONCEPT
The two interlocking circles represent the senior and the caregiver. The coral
heart sitting in their overlap is the bond between them — the care your app
exists to support.

WHAT'S INSIDE

  symbol/                 The mark alone, transparent background.
                          Use when you need just the icon (favicon, social
                          avatar, watermark on screenshots).
                          symbol_1024.png — large/print
                          symbol_512.png  — web/social
                          symbol_192.png  — small UI

  app_icon/               The mark inside a sage squircle.
                          Use as the phone home-screen icon.
                          app_icon_1024.png — App Store / Play Store
                          app_icon_512.png  — high-DPI displays
                          app_icon_192.png  — Android xxxhdpi
                          app_icon_48.png   — small launcher / notification

  wordmark/               Symbol + "ElderAssist" together.
                          wordmark_horizontal.png       — primary, light bg
                          wordmark_horizontal_dark.png  — on dark bg
                          wordmark_horizontal_mono.png  — single-color print
                          wordmark_stacked.png          — square spaces

  svg_source/             All vectors, infinitely scalable.
                          Use these for the poster, the report cover, any
                          large-format print. Open in Illustrator, Figma,
                          Inkscape, or any browser.

  brand_sheet.png         The full kit on one page (for reference).


COLORS

  Sage         #3F7D5C    primary
  Sage dark    #2C5942    gradient base, dark variant background
  Coral        #E87B5A    accent (the heart)
  Cream        #FBF7F0    background canvas
  Ink          #1A2B22    body text


TYPOGRAPHY IN THE WORDMARK

  Headline   Fraunces, weight 500 (the "Elder" part is weight 600)
  Caps lock  Inter, weight 400, 3pt letter-spacing


SPACING (CLEAR SPACE)

When placing the wordmark next to other elements, keep a margin around it
equal to the height of the symbol. Don't crowd it.


WHEN TO USE WHICH

  Anywhere the app's name appears in text             →  wordmark_horizontal
  Phone home screen, Play Store / App Store           →  app_icon
  Report cover, business card, poster header          →  wordmark_horizontal
  Square social-media avatar, small UI badge          →  symbol or app_icon
  University documents that print in B&W              →  wordmark_horizontal_mono
